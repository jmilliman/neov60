Vibrant Skin Blend 30ml

This blend is for mature women who want to take the best care of their skin. It is made with Kalahari melon seed oil, pomegranate oil, acai seed oil, red raspberry, sea buckthorn, guava, prickly pear and neroli. It smells divinely of delicate orange blossom, one of the most expensive and therapeutically used essential oils for the skin. This blend deeply moisturizes, softens skin and improves skin tone. It evens skin tone and fades age spots over time. It is amazing for dry skin. It tightens saggy skin and smoothes wrinkles. It helps restore cellular damage caused by oxidation. It is very emollient and is high in Omega 3, 6, 9. It is also high in vitamins A, C and E. Vibrant skin helps regenerate healthier skin over time and restores tired, depleted skin.

Scar Tissue Blend 15ml

Here is a wonderful gift from the plant world. I use premium wildcrafted rose geranium to soften hardened tissue; wild crafted lemongrass to send the waste out through the lymph system and organic orange oil, jojoba and foraha to create healthy new tissue. This blend is amazing.

I have had dozens of clients use this for hardened lymph, muscle tissue, muscle tears, striated tissue, and hardened fat cells, all with great results. I was my own first customer when I used this recipe on myself. In a few short weeks, my breast tissue, which had come to feel striated and hardened, softened and the lymph cleared where I no longer felt any congestion or restrictions. To be clear, I am not suggesting this as an answer to breast cancer. This is suited to situations where there is newly hardened, striated, blocked tissue or congestion. If you are unsure about your situation, seek medical guidance before using.

I have used it on people with recent surgeries, where the wound was closed over and in active healing. It softens the tissue and prevents tissue build-up and restrictions in movement. It keeps the lymph moving so there is not discomfort from blockage, and it speeds the healing process. It smells lovely, too!
