Antiviral Blend 5ml

Thyme thymol and Blue Gum Eucalyptus are very effective against viral infections, strep and bacterial infections. Usage: At the first sign of a runny nose, sore throat or cough, put 5 drops in a diffuser and run it for 15 minutes 2x per day, morning and night. This can be used for children as well, though protect children from putting their mouths over or near the diffuser opening.

You may also apply it to the throat and chest area as follows:

For ages 10-Adult: mix 10 drops of antiviral blend in 1 teaspoon of carrier oil, and apply it to the neck and chest 2-3x per day until all symptoms are relieved.

For children 5-10: put 3-6 drops in 1 teaspoon of carrier oil and apply it to the neck and chest 2-3x per day until all symptoms are relieved.

For ages 1-4: mix 2 drops in 1 teaspoon of carrier oil and apply it to the bottoms of their feet.

This is not intended to replace medical advice or diagnose or cure disease.
