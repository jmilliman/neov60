Lighten Up

Fat Burning & Lymph Cleansing Massage Blends

Our look becomes most apparent during summer. As the clothes come off, all that was hidden under winter weight clothing becomes quite obvious! We have an inner voice that tells us that taking care of our selves year round is the best lifestyle. So rather than feeling bad, use this summer as an opportunity to change your diet and exercise plan, and use these oils to become your personal best year round!

Please note that citrus oils are phytotoxic. This means that they can make you more sensitive to sunburn when used before going outside. It's better to wear sunscreen for protection in the sun. it is most effective to use these blends before or after your shower, and after your time in the sun.

Fat Burning Massage Blend

10 drops Pink Grapefruit

5 drops Lemon

5 drops Bitter Orange

5 drops Cypress

5 drops Lime

5 drops Bay Laurel (optional)

4 oz. of Coconut, Olive, Jojoba or Almond oil (Carrier Oil)

Mix together the essential oils and then add the mixture to the carrier oil. Apply lightly to the skin from ankle to the inside of the thighs, where the lymph gathers. Then move up around the groin to the belly and rub in a gentle, clockwise, circular motion from the bottom of the rib cage to the top of the pelvic bone. Apply from the neck down to the collarbones; and from the wrists, up the arms, and to the armpits.

Drink plenty of water, as the lymph and toxic waste will move through the body fluids. Lemon water is particularly helpful for flushing wastes without headache or stomach upset.
